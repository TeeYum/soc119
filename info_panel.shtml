Info:

This is a custom web-based app that leverages Twitter or Instagram for back channel conversations about "You Can't Say That," a television series about the work of Sam Richards, Laurie Mulvey and the team of facilitators at World in Conversation at Penn State University.

You Can't Say That - Trailer.

In the future, Soc119 will be filmed as part of a television series. Prior to that, this site will be used as a pilot for the app that will accompany the television program.
